History & Fun Facts

Discover the story, culture, and hidden history of Barangay Camanlangan

Origin of Camanlangan

Barangay Camanlangan is known for its rich forests, mountain landscapes, and peaceful environment. The place became known because of its beautiful natural scenery and welcoming local community.

Nature and Mountains

The barangay is surrounded by mountains and green forests, making it one of the hidden eco-tourism gems in New Bataan, Davao de Oro.

Local Culture

Camanlangan celebrates local traditions through community gatherings, farming festivals, and nature appreciation activities.

Fun Fact

Many visitors are amazed by the cool weather, scenic mountain views, and peaceful atmosphere that make Camanlangan perfect for relaxation.
