About Explore Camlon

Your guide to the beauty of Barangay Camanlangan

What is Explore Camlon?

Explore Camlon is a web-based tourism and information guide designed to promote the beauty and attractions of Barangay Camanlangan, New Bataan, Davao de Oro.

This platform allows visitors to explore scenic destinations, discover local culture, and access useful travel information about the barangay.

Mission

To promote Barangay Camanlangan as a tourism destination by providing accessible and reliable information about its attractions, culture, and environment.

Vision

To become a trusted digital tourism guide that showcases the beauty of Camanlangan and encourages sustainable tourism.

About Barangay Camanlangan

Barangay Camanlangan, located in New Bataan, Davao de Oro,
is a peaceful destination blessed with breathtaking landscapes,
rich greenery, and refreshing natural scenery.
