Our History

The Legacy of
Camanlangan

The story of Barangay Camanlangan is one of deep-rooted perseverance. Its name is derived from the inspiring phrase “kamang lang, bahalag malangan,” which serves as a timeless reminder that progress, no matter how slow, is still moving forward.

01

Meaning

"Kamang lang, bahalag malangan" translated literally means to crawl or move slow, as long as you eventually arrive at your destination.

02

Founding Day

Officially established in 1982, the community celebrates its anniversary every November 11.

03

Legacy

This history honors the humble beginnings and the steady, peaceful development of the community over the decades.
