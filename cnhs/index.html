Camanlangan National High School

Camanlangan National High School is a place of learning and community engagement, dedicated to providing quality secondary education to the youth of the barangay. It serves as a vital institution for academic and personal development.

📍 Location

P-4 Camanlangan, New Bataan

🌤️ Best Time to Visit

Weekdays during school hours and during various school-led community activities.
